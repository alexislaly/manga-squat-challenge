Become a legendary warrior by doing squats in augmented reality!

How does it work?

  1. Start the app with the START button
  2. Allow the application to access the camera
  3. Point the camera at a person (the one who will be squating). The person must be visible from head to toe.
  4. Your turn to play: SQUAT ! SQUAT ! SQUAT ! SQUAT ! SQUAT !

Watch the progression as the counter increases and become a legendary warrior!

Take a screenshot and share it on social media with the hashtags #mangasquatchallenge and @bureims

This project was developed as part of the Sport and Manga event, organized by the University Library of the University of Reims Champagne-Ardenne.

Info

No personal data and no images are stored by the application.
